== Description ==
Send email using wordpress dashboard enables you to send emails from your wordpress dashboard. Here you can enter email id of your friends, type subject of your email and the body of 
your email. You will be able to send the email to your friends


== Installation ==

* Download "lordlinus-send-email.zip" to your computer.
* Login as administrator
* Click on Plugins --> Add New
* On "Install Plugins" page, clik on "Upload" 
* Browse and select "send-email-using-wordpress-repositoy.zip" and click on "Install Now"
* You can activate it now
* Go to setting of the page and enter the required field
* now you are able to send to send mails

Important : Check OPENSSL of your server is on.
